CIRCUS ON THE CANAL

28.10 - 28.11.2021

UNIVERSITY OF ANTWEP, BELGIUM

KLOOSTER VAN DE GRAUWZUSTERS

LANG SINT-ANNASTRAAT, 2000 ANTWERP

The series “Circus on the canal” is a socio-political project by Homa Arkani based in Gent, Belgium. She has been working on this project from 2019.
There would be a reception on opening of the exhibition at Klooster van de grauwzusters on October 28. If you want to participate in the reception, please contact Homa Arkani from contact forum of this website.
